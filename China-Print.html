China Print 2017!
HP Graphics Solution Business
Experience the latest in printing tech knowledge and interior decoration innovations at the HP Booth.
This year, HP is returning to China Print 2017 to showcase the latest in our cutting-edge portfolio of print solutions from 9 to 13 May 2017.

There are many exciting activities lined up for you, like workshops and live demonstrations across our range of solutions. Take a walk through our corrugated packaging gallery to see our packaging innovations come to life. Or open your eyes to the latest creative concepts in our Latex interior décor showroom! Visitors at the HP booth will experience how, in a digital world, print remains a key growth driver for Print Service Providers and their brand customers.

Join us to learn how you can adopt the latest technologies and creative concepts for faster, more sustainable, and more inspiring design solutions that will set you above your competitors.

We look forward to seeing you at China Print 2017 – register your presence or book a demo timeslot now.
